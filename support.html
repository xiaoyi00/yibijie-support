Quick fixes

Bluetooth pairing

Make sure Bluetooth is enabled, the device is in pairing mode, and the app has Bluetooth permission.

AI chat not responding

Check your network, sign out and sign in again, then retry. If it persists, email support.

Subscription issues

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Verify the Apple ID purchase history and restore purchases.

Login problems

Confirm your account and password. Use the reset password flow if needed.

Sync or data missing

Pull to refresh. If data is still missing, contact support with your account info.

Reporting a bug

Send steps to reproduce, screenshots, and time of the issue to help us investigate.
